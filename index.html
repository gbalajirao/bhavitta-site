What it does

One model for your whole financial life

From your US career years to a retirement abroad — income, taxes, a home, kids' college, social security, and one-time events — all on a single interactive timeline.

Life-stage timeline

Map every chapter as a stage — US HCOL/MCOL, a foreign city (30+ curated), college, social security, owning or renting. Gaps auto-fill so the model never breaks.

Two simulation models

Replay real US market history (1928–2023, nominal or Shiller-real) or run 10,000 Monte Carlo trials. Both give you a probability fan and your true survival rate.

Stress-test heatmap

A return × inflation grid shows exactly how sensitive your plan is to the two assumptions that move the needle most.

Year-by-year ledger

An amortization view of resolved cash flow — income, taxes, expenses, college, property, one-time events, and closing balance — for any percentile path.

Compare & share scenarios

Save plans side by side against a feasibility benchmark. Clone, rename, share an image, or export as Markdown for an AI second opinion.

Private by design

Everything is stored locally with SwiftData. No accounts, no analytics, no servers — your numbers never leave your phone. Localized in 20 currencies.

How it works

Three steps to a real forecast

STEP 1

Set your inputs

Your age, starting corpus, expected return, inflation, and household size — with sensible cross-border defaults.

STEP 2

Build your timeline

Add the places you'll live and the events along the way. Each stage's income and spending roll up automatically.

STEP 3

Read the outcome

See a fan of projected balances, your survival odds, and a stress test — then tune and compare scenarios.

Privacy

Your money stays on your device

Bhavitta is built to be useful without ever asking you to trust it with your data.

No data collection

The app collects no personal data and includes no analytics or third-party trackers.

No network calls

All computation runs on-device. Your plan is stored locally with SwiftData and is never uploaded.

You control sharing

Exporting a scenario or a share-image is always explicit, through the standard iOS share sheet you initiate.

In short: there is no account to create, nothing is tracked, and nothing leaves your phone unless you explicitly share it.
